About PulseBlog

PulseBlog is a student-built concept for a modern blog app landing experience. It focuses on clear sections, conversion-focused CTAs, and reusable content components.

Modern UI

Dark theme, soft gradients, and professional spacing system.

Readable Content

Typography-first approach designed for long-form articles.

Reusable Blocks

Sections and cards can be reused across future pages.
